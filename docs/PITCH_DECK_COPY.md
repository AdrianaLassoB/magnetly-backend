# Magnetly pitch deck copy

## Slide 1
Magnetly
AI marketing intelligence for fast growing D2C home goods brands

## Slide 2
Problem
Fast growing D2C home goods brands collect reviews, support feedback, orders, and marketing signals across disconnected tools. As they grow, spreadsheets and separate dashboards stop being enough.

## Slide 3
Target customer
Magnetly is built for fast rising D2C home goods brands that need one place to understand what customers complain about, what products to improve, and where to invest marketing budget.

## Slide 4
Solution
Magnetly turns scattered customer and product signals into product level insight, trend tracking, and AI recommendations.
